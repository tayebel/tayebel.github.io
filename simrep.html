Reservoir Simulation Report Tool
Mai 2023-Ongoing
Introduction

In the realm of reservoir engineering, time is of the essence, particularly when it comes to reporting simulation results. Reservoir engineers often find themselves in search of different Excel templates to present their findings efficiently. To address this challenge and save valuable time, we have developed a Python-based tool that automates the generation of reservoir simulation Excel reports, specifically designed for Resinsight software.

Features
  • This tool leverages the advanced capabilities of Resinsight software, a widely acclaimed software utilized for reservoir visualization and analysis. Through seamless integration with Resinsight, this tool effortlessly extracts the essential data needed to generate comprehensive reports. It encompasses a wide range of crucial information, including well details, fluid characteristics, water, oil and gas production and injection rates from each well, hydrocarbons initially present, and pressure measurements. With our tool, the arduous process of manual data extraction and intricate formatting becomes a thing of the past.
  • For now the tool can generate one template of reservoir simulation Excel report.
