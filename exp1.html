Freelance Work: Developing Black Oil Models

Introduction

As a freelance professional, I specialize in developing black oil models using Eclipse data files and reporting the results using ResInsight software. I work closely with clients to provide comprehensive solutions for reservoir simulation and analysis. My expertise lies in utilizing OPM open source software for running simulations and leveraging ResInsight for visualizing and interpreting the simulation results. By offering my services as a freelancer, I ensure personalized and tailored solutions that meet the specific needs of my clients.

Tasks

  • Utilizing OPM

    I employ the OPM open source software to run simulations for black oil models. This software provides advanced capabilities for modeling fluid flow and transport in porous media, allowing for accurate and efficient simulations.

  • Visualizing Results with ResInsight

    I utilize ResInsight software to visualize and analyze the simulation results. ResInsight offers comprehensive visualization tools that enable detailed representations of pressure distribution, saturation profiles, and production forecasts.

  • Sensitivity Studies

    I conduct sensitivity studies to evaluate the impact of varying key parameters on the performance of black oil models. This helps optimize reservoir management strategies and identify the most effective parameters for enhanced oil recovery.

  • Pilot Models for EOR Techniques

    I build pilot models specifically designed for enhanced oil recovery (EOR) techniques, including CO2 injection, water alternating gas (WAG), and waterflooding. These pilot models simulate the application of these techniques in real reservoir scenarios, aiding in assessing their feasibility and effectiveness.

Skills

  • Expertise in OPM and ResInsight
  • Sensitivity Analysis
  • EOR Modeling
